WORK SYSTEM DESIGN BEHAVIORS

Build your continuous improvement muscles!

We equip your teams with the
5 Key Behaviors of Rapid Process Improvement.

They include:

1

Use key performance metrics to identify which products or services to improve.

2

When products or services break down, seek to understand the root cause.

3

Design experiments to improve products or services within a specific time frame.

4

Iteratively improve key products or services based on data from experiments.

5

Seek feedback from the team's stakeholders to make sure they are meeting their needs.

The 7 Steps of Lean Process Improvement

In this transformational sprint, we'll guide your organization through the 7 steps of lean process improvement.

1

Select a process that is in need of improvement.

2

Map the current process.

3

Select the “pain point” that is causing the most pain.

4

Generate a list of actions that solve the root problem.

5

Implement measurable improvements and learnings on key metrics.

6

Measure results.

7

Develop and implement training on the new standard work.
