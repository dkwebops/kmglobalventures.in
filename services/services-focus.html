5 Key Behaviors of Agile Strategic Planning.

They include:

1

Set goals that focus on desired outcomes for your stakeholders.

2

Identify and track performance metrics that lead to desired outcomes.

3

Align goals and metrics cross-functionally across the organization.

4

Talk about potential obstacles to goals and devise strategies to overcome them.

5

Appropriately scope the resources and time needed to achieve goals.

We customize our offerings to meet your needs

Depending on the needs of your organization, we'll draw from a variety of strategic planning tools, such as:

Hoshin Kanri

Hoshin Kanri (aka Strategy Deployment) is a tried and true lean management method for aligning your company strategy across all three levels of the organization (executive, cross-functional, and frontline teams). 

Agile OKRs

An Agile Approach to OKRs (Objectives and Key Results) is an evolved approach to Andy Grove's original formulation that helps organizations more effectively plan and execute in complex environments. 

Performance Measurement

Performance measurement is all about getting clarity on the indicators of success for your organization across all levels of the organization - and getting good at tracking strategy against those indicators.
